Wo mujhse nafrat karti hai...

Wo mujhse nafrat karti hai hai
chalo isi bahne uske jindigi me
mera bhi ek kirdar hai.
Wo dil se nahi
dimag se ada karne wali gazab ki adakar hai.

unki nafrate chahe jis wajah se ho
par wo mere pyar ki haqdar hai
aisa pahli baar nhi hua hai mera unke sath
ha ye nafrat unka mere liye har bar hai.

ankhon ko wo pher leti hai
jab unhe mera chehra dikhta bhi ek baar hai
unke dil me ked hamari jhuti nafrate
ek labj ki kahani nahi
wo to mil ke hajar hai.

Haa wo mujhse nafrate karti hai
chalo isi bahne unke dil me
mera bhi ek kirdar hai.

Mera gawn

Dekho mera gawn mujhe kaise bula raha hai
Dada ji ka kandhe Dadi ka dular
Papa ki dant Maa ka pyar
Sab ek hi palko me dikha raha hai
Dekho kaise mera gawn mujhhe mere bachpan ka
yaad dila raha hai.

sauk se ruthna, maa ko tang karna
bahan se hamesa ladna
Sab ek hi palko me dikha raha hai
dekho kaise mera gawn mujhhe mere bachpan ki
yaad dila raha hai.

Wo bachpan ki yariyan, wo aam ki bagicha
hostel jane ki aanakani sab ek hi palko me dikha raha hai
dekho kaise mera gawn mujhhe mere bachpan ki yad dila raha hai.

Nind

Ye nind jara to so lene do
use aise na par sapne me to sath reh lene do....

Khabon ki duniya

Khabon ki duniya bhi badi ajib hoti
hai do pal ki hi sahi par pas sab kuchh
hoti hai....

Pyar

Koi to jadu hai unke pyar me
warna youn hi nahi gujrata itne din
unke intjar me.....

Soul

Always try to listen your soul instead of
your heart because sometimes your heart may
be wrong but your soul will always right....
